## Contributing to Related Projects

If iTCR development yields insights applicable to related projects:

1. **Document Finding:**
   - What was discovered
   - How it applies to both iTCR and related project
   - Evidence/data supporting the finding

2. **Propose Integration:**
   - Open issue or discussion in related project
   - Describe potential benefit
   - Offer collaboration or contribution

3. **Implement Collaboratively:**
   - Work with maintainers of related project
   - Ensure bidirectional benefit
   - Document integration

4. **Maintain Connection:**
   - Update both projects as needed
   - Keep integration documentation current
   - Share results and learnings
